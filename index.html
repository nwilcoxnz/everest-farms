Everest Farms New Zealand is a family-owned business known for producing export quality beef and lamb.

At Everest Farms we raise, produce, source and supply premium export quality beef and lamb to meet the high standards we set for ourselves and those of our partner clients. Our beef and lamb have been raised on New Zealand’s 100% natural grass pastures and our animals live in a natural stress-free environment.

Being from one of the furthest corners of the world brings it own challenges but as a nation with the Kiwi “number 8 wire mentality” and a can-do attitude New Zealand has overcome all obstacles. Our isolation in geographic terms means that our food chain is not compromised by the harmful effects of pollution and our clean green environment make our products taste unique. Our products are rich and full of flavour but they also contain the delicate taste of the subtilties and flavour of the natural environment in which our animals are raised. Our meat is lean, clean and green and coming “from the mountains to the sea” we can deliver anywhere in the world. Our meat processing partner carries all the international accreditations necessary to comply with international standards including Halal certification. We are also able to source and supply export quality fresh produce and seafood to meet your requirements.

Family-owned

In 2002, the Haynes family purchased Everest Farm located in the foot hills of Puketapu, in the Hawkes Bay of New Zealand. The Hawkes Bay region is highly regarded worldwide for the quality of its beef, lamb, fresh produce, wine and tourism.

Trade Partnerships

The New Zealand Singapore Closer Economic Partnership, New Zealand’s Free Trade Agreement with Malaysia and the Hong Kong/China Closer Economic Partnership has opened up a new era for Everest Farms to supply first class export quality beef and Lamb to our clients in Asia and around the world.

Investment in New Zealand

At Everest Farms we are uniquely positioned to assist and facilitate overseas investment in New Zealand. This is in the form of Farm partnerships and other business opportunities through our own directors’ knowledge and experience as well as through our network of professional advisors.

We look forward to you joining the Everest Farms family.
